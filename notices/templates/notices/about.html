About the Noticeboard

The College Digital Noticeboard is an initiative to streamline and digitize all academic and administrative announcements across departments. It provides students with a single, reliable source for schedules, exam alerts, event notifications, and official announcements.

Key Features

  • **Real-time Updates:** Announcements are available instantly.
  • **Filtered Archive:** Easily search and filter historical notices by department or semester.
  • **Secure Posting:** Only authorized faculty members can post notices.
  • **Multi-File Support:** Teachers can attach multiple documents to a single announcement.

Version 1.0.0 | Developed by Inderjeet Singh [B.Tech. from LIET]
